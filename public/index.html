Rules & Guildlines


Show Respect

No bullying, harassment or racism. Avoid cussing as much as possible and absolutely no hate ideologies.


Spam Prohibited

Don't spam anything such as messages, emojis or files. Avoid self-promo as much as possible.


Safe Content

Illegal contents are strictly prohibited. We may allow NSFW content but not if it was meant literally.


Channel Uses

Use the right channels properly. Intentionally misusing channels would result in muting or in extreme case, timeout.


No Impersonation

Impersonating staffs or abusing alts is not permitted. For staff, do not abuse your authority. Violating this rules may result in timeout or immediate ban, even real life legal actions.


Follow Staff

Follow staffs order. You can argue if it's unreasonable, but not disobeying.


Protect Privacy

Protect your privacy. Do not share or try to get others personal info. Doxxing will result in timeout or even ban.


Misuse of Bots

Use bots correctly and in the right channel. Intentionally violating will result in punishments.


Avoid Brainrot

Brainrot is strictly prohibited. It's our culture from the OG server. Violating this might result in violations of both this rule and rule 1 of being respectful.


No Exploitations

Report loopholes right away and do not exploit it. These contains: phrasing/wording problem, logical problem, etc


Justice Obstruction

Obstruction of Justice like denying even though you are a direct witness or destruction of evidence will all be considered as Obstruction of Justice


No Tolerance Policy

Actions have consequences. Violate the rules and you pay for it. No tolerance.


Context Matters

Context matter. We might not punish you if your message/bio/avatar violate a rule but not targeting anyone specifically. Multiple violations will still result in punishment.


Legal Disclaimer

We are responsible for direct and indirect damages only when clear and compelling evidence shows that we failed to take action to address a violation after it was reported to us. Such evidence may include, among other things, third-party testimony or legally verified images or videos.
